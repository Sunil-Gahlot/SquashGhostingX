Our Story

Built by players,
for every player.

Every serious squash player knows the feeling. You finish forty minutes of solo ghosting — sweating, lungs burning, every position covered. You did the work. But walking off court, a quiet question follows you: was any of that actually making me better? Varish and Darshika Gahlot spent years asking that question on courts they had entirely to themselves, before dawn. Squash GhostingX is the answer they built — and decided to give away for free.

The Beginning

The question every solo
player stops asking

Solo squash training has a problem nobody talks about: you can be disciplined, consistent, and genuinely hard-working — and still have no honest picture of whether any of it is moving your game forward. Ghosting drills get done. Positions get covered. Sweat gets produced. But without feedback, without data, there is no way to separate effort from progress. The drill that felt sharp on Tuesday might be reinforcing the same weak pattern it always has. You simply have no way to know.

That was Varish's world for years. On court at 6am, before the club opened, running ghost patterns with the kind of focus most players never find. The commitment was never in question. What was missing was any way to know whether it was working — which zones were weak, where pace was fading, whether this session's movement quality was actually sharper than last month's. At the professional level, coaches, video analysts, and movement data answer exactly these questions. For the rest of the squash world, you train on instinct and hope.

"I wanted the data that professional coaches have — position coverage, pace consistency, zone balance — in a format I could use alone, at 6am, on any court in the world. That tool didn't exist. So we built it."

— Varish Gahlot, Co-Founder & CEO

Together with Darshika, Varish designed Squash GhostingX from the court up — not as a fitness timer with a squash label, but as a genuine training intelligence system built around how ghosting actually works: structured position sequencing, pace progression, zone-weighted sessions, and session-over-session analytics that show you the truth about your own movement over time.

When the app launched in 2025, the decision to make it completely free was never a marketing strategy. It was the whole point. Elite training tools have always been gated by money, access, and geography. Squash GhostingX was built to remove all three barriers — for every player, at every level, on every court in the world.

Vision & Mission

What we're building
and why it matters

Ghosting is the foundation of squash fitness. Every elite player does it. Most club players know they should do more of it. The gap between knowing and actually doing — structured, measurable, progressive ghosting — is exactly where Squash GhostingX lives.

Our vision is a world where every squash player, regardless of budget or access to coaching, has the tools to train like a professional. That means an AI voice coach that calls your positions, analytics that show your weak zones over time, structured programmes built on movement science, and a session history that gives you honest data about your own development.

The best training tools shouldn't come with a price tag.
Squash GhostingX is free forever.

Every court system. Every programme. AI voice in every language. Unlimited history. Full analytics. No trial, no subscription, no ads — for every player, at every level, forever. That's the founding commitment and it isn't changing.

What We Stand For

Four things we don't
compromise on

🎾
Court-First Thinking

Every feature is designed around what actually happens during a solo session — not what looks good in a product demo. If it doesn't make your training better on court, it doesn't ship.

📊
Honest Data

Your analytics show what's real: which zones you avoid, where your pace drops, how your consistency shifts over weeks. We don't smooth the numbers to make you feel better. You improve faster when you see the truth.

🔓
Free, Always

Not free as a trial. Not free for basic features with a paywall on the rest. Free: every court system, every programme, full analytics, unlimited history. No conditions, no expiry, no exceptions.

📴
Offline by Design

Courts rarely have good Wi-Fi. The app runs entirely on your device — no server check-ins, no loading screens, no connectivity requirement. Open the app, set your session, and go.
