6-Point & 10-Point

Master the six.
Earn the ten.

We built this because
serious players deserve better
than a kitchen timer.

Every elite squash player shares the same secret: thousands of hours alone on a court, sprinting to corners that hold no ball, playing strokes against empty walls. Ghosting. The most brutally effective solo drill in racquet sport. And for decades, the most intelligent thing coaching it was a stopwatch.

No voice to call positions. No data on which zones were being avoided. No feedback on whether backhand recovery was slower at minute 25 than minute 5. No structure beyond “keep going.” The effort was real. The intelligence wasn’t there.

Squash GhostingX gives every solo session a coach, a structure, and a memory. So you train smart — not just hard. And because the players who put in the work shouldn’t pay for the tools to do it, the full app is free. For every player, at every level, on every court in the world. Now and permanently.

Ghosting is a solo drill where you sprint to each court position and play a shadow stroke — as if the ball were there — then recover to the T. It’s also one of the best aerobic conditioning drills in any racquet sport.
