The Science

Why Ghosting Builds Champions

Ghosting — moving to every court corner without the ball — forges explosive footwork, match-level aerobic fitness, and the tactical instincts that separate good players from great ones. The world's top squash coaches prescribe it every training day.

🧠

Motor Pattern Automaticity

Court movement becomes instinctive through deliberate repetition. Your split-step fires before your brain decides, your lunge depth extends beyond what feels possible, and your recovery line becomes a reflex — not a decision.

⚡

Explosive Agility & Directional Change

Ghosting trains the exact energy systems squash demands: explosive first steps, lateral deceleration, multi-directional speed, and the aerobic capacity to sustain all of it across a five-game match.

📍

Dominate the T — Every Rep

Every ghost ends at the T. Not sometimes — every single time. The app enforces this until owning the T is as automatic as breathing. On match day, the player who controls the T controls the rally.

🎤

Shot Simulation Intelligence

AI calls positions alongside shot types — "Front Left, Drop Shot" — training your mind to prepare the stroke during movement, not after arrival. Tactical IQ built at speed, hands-free, every rep.

Move With Purpose.
Think Under Pressure.

Pure movement drills train your body. Shot simulation drills train your mind. SquashGhostingX combines both — calling a court position and a shot type simultaneously so you're mentally preparing the stroke while your legs are still carrying you there. By match day, the connection is automatic.
